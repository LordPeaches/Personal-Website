StealthBot

StealthBot was a project I started in order to develop a better understanding of FIRST Robotics Robot Design, CAD Practices, and Workflow.

In this project, I took a few students along with me and we spend a lot of time designing and redesigning a "Ideal robot" for this years FRC Game. In the process, we learned a lot not only about Robot Design, but the workflow and how to manage tradeoffs.

I proceeded to CAD approximately 95% of this robot using OnShape, and then we rendered it using Fusion360 and Blender.
