StealthBot: FRC Concept Robot

A full-fidelity FRC robot CAD project designed to sharpen design workflow, system integration, and tradeoff analysis.

StealthBot was a self-initiated project to push my FRC robot design skills beyond mentorship constraints. Working with a small group of students, we set out to design the most competitive robot possible for that year's FRC game, without the time pressure of a real build season.

The process involved iterative design, subsystem layout tradeoffs, and extensive collaboration on mechanism selection. I CAD'd approximately 95% of the robot in OnShape, then produced high-fidelity renderings in Fusion 360 and Blender for presentation and review.

The result was a fully detailed, competition-ready robot concept, and a significantly improved understanding of how to architect complex mechanical systems under real-world constraints.
