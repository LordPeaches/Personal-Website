Meet FIRST Robotics Team 612 "Chantilly Robotics"

I first met FRC Team 612 in the Fall of 2019 after connecting with one of their mentors who worked at GMU, and I instantly got sucked in to their team's culture and passion.

FRC Team 612, based out of Chantilly High School, is the largest FIRST Robotics Team in the Chesapeake District, actively pulling 130+ students each year. Their spirit and enthusiasm is unmatched by any other team.

I originally joined their team to help their Marketing students design and edit Videos, but now I serve as their CAD mentor, teaching students how to CAD using OnShape, as well as serving as their Drive Coach, teaching students the basics of Strategy and preparing them for the competition.
