Robotics Mentorship

FRC Team 612: Chantilly Robotics

CAD mentor and Drive Coach for the largest FIRST Robotics team in the Chesapeake District.

I first connected with FRC Team 612 in Fall 2019 through a mentor who worked at GMU, and was immediately pulled in by the team's culture and level of commitment. Team 612, based out of Chantilly High School in Northern Virginia, is the largest FIRST Robotics team in the Chesapeake District with 130+ active students per season.

I joined initially to help with marketing and video production, but quickly moved into a technical mentorship role. I now serve as their CAD mentor, teaching students mechanical design and assembly workflow in OnShape, and as Drive Coach, preparing students for competition strategy, alliance coordination, and match execution.

Working with a team this large reinforced my ability to communicate engineering concepts at different levels, manage competing priorities, and scale technical mentorship across a broad group of students with varying skill levels.
