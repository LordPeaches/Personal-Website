Engineering & Entrepreneurship

Hypernova Solar, Founder & President

Built the first new solar car team in Virginia in nearly 20 years, from a freshman's idea to a 40-member 501(c)(3).

Hypernova Solar is an independent 501(c)(3) non-profit organization based in Northern Virginia, where a team of university students designs, builds, tests, and competes with fully solar-powered electric vehicles in international competition.

I founded the team as a freshman in Fall 2017. No one at George Mason University had heard of solar car racing. Within three years, I grew the organization to 40+ active members, secured $25,000+ in sponsorships, converted the club into a registered non-profit, and delivered a completed, competition-ready solar car, the first new solar car built in Virginia in nearly 20 years.

This project required me to operate simultaneously as an engineer, executive director, fundraiser, and recruiter. It's where I first developed the cross-functional leadership and program management skills I would later apply in aerospace.
