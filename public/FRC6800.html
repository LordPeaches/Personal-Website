Robotics Mentorship

FRC Team 6800 Valor: Texas State Champions

Mentored the #7-ranked robotics team in the world through their championship season.

After relocating to Austin, I sought out a local FRC team to mentor. I connected with Team 6800 Valor from Vandergrift High School, a young team with an exceptional group of student engineers and knowledgeable mentors who were clearly building something special.

The team's engineering rigor was unlike anything I'd seen at the high school level. Their students were genuinely doing the design work, not just being guided through it, and the results showed. We went undefeated at our first competition, won our second, and dominated the Texas UIL State Championships, earning the first-ever UIL State Robotics award. Then we went to FIRST World Championships, where we were ranked #7 in the world.

Mentoring Valor reshaped how I think about engineering culture, talent development, and what's achievable with a focused team and a high standard. It was one of the most rewarding technical experiences of my career.
