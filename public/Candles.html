Candles — A Full-Stack Marketing Experiment

Taught myself web development, built a payment-enabled e-commerce site from scratch, and ran a social media marketing campaign — all in a summer.

In Summer 2019, a friend needed a website and marketing infrastructure for his candle business. I treated it as a self-directed crash course: I taught myself HTML/CSS, built a complete website from scratch, integrated payment processing, and launched social media channels — generating hundreds of followers and thousands of impressions within weeks.

The venture ultimately wound down, and the lesson was clear: passion is a prerequisite for a successful startup. I wasn't invested in the product itself, and without that drive, it was hard to sustain the effort beyond the initial technical build.

What I kept from the experience: hands-on competency in front-end development and digital marketing, and a deeper appreciation for founder-market fit as a requirement — not a nice-to-have.
