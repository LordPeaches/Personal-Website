Candles Sales: An Experiment in Marketing

Sometimes the best way to learn is to just throw yourself in the deep end. I am a big believer in the 'Sink or Swim' method of teaching, even when it comes to teaching myself.

In the Summer of 2019, my friend asked me to help him start a business. He liked making candles, and he needed a Website and Marketing platform. I taught myself HTML/CSS, built a website from scratch, and hooked the website up to Firebase, and got it to accept payments. I also built him social media outlets, and generated hundreds of followers and thousands of impressions.

Why'd it fail?

I learned a great lesson about Entreprneurship: If you aren't passionate about your project, it won't succeed. I wasn't passionate about my friends candle business, and I slowly pulled away from it to work on more enjoyable projects (Such as my Belt Printer Project).
